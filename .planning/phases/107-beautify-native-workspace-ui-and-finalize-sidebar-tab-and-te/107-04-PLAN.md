<interfaces>
`createSnapshotBuilder(dependencies)` currently exposes `buildWorkspace`, `buildAll`, `currentRevision`, and `resolveNativeLaunch`; `SnapshotDependencies.listWorkspaceNames()` is the enumeration seam.

`FileSnapshotRevisionStore.update(digest)` increments only when the current digest changes. Calling it with `digest([])` is therefore sufficient to assign and advance the empty aggregate revision.

`invalidateConfigCache()` clears both entity maps and populated-list flags. Ordinary CLI/TUI reads retain `listWorkspaces()` cache behavior; the service gets an explicit uncached entry point.
</interfaces>
