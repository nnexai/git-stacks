A record must remain charged exactly once while queued, reserved, encoded, and awaiting transport: transfer its existing charge atomically from broker-owned queue to bridge-owned reservation without a moment of double charging or zero charging; release it only after a positive-capacity stream pull successfully enqueues the frame for runtime/socket delivery. A failed enqueue keeps/terminates the reservation and is never acknowledged. The subscriber's configured maxEvents/maxBytes governs the combined broker-queue plus bridge-reservation totals. Expose read-only broker, bridge, and combined pending event/byte counters, caps, and closed/disconnect cursor state for diagnostics/tests. Tests may observe counters and inject smaller caps, but may not invoke pull, mutate desiredSize, acknowledge frames, or otherwise manufacture demand. Heartbeats share bounded bridge capacity but do not consume the event ledger or advance the event cursor.
